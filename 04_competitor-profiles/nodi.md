## Positioning & Messaging

**Primary value proposition**: A personalized AI tutor on the Georgian national curriculum that assesses a student, finds gaps, and adapts in real time. It democratizes what used to require an expensive private tutor (რეპეტიტორი).

**Target audience (now)**: Middle & upper-grade school students (≈ grades V-XII). Serves both strugglers/exam-preppers AND high achievers wanting more. Parents are the buyer; the demo funnel is "school or family."

**Positioning angle**: "Not just another chatbot." Differentiates from ChatGPT on: curriculum-grounded (authorized textbooks), real-time adaptation, diagnoses WHY a student fails and fills foundational gaps. "First Georgian adaptive learning platform."

**Key messaging themes**:
- Personalization / adapts to each child's pace (homepage, parents)
- Replaces costly private tutors, available 24/7 (parents)
- Transparency for parents: dual parent+child accounts, real-time progress (founder interview)
- Curriculum + expert credibility (textbook-based, math-teacher advisors)
- Founder story: named after CEO's little brother "Nodi," a natural self-directed learner
