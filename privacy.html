Privacy Policy

Last updated: 8 June 2026

Core principle: NikoLearn runs entirely on your child's device. The app requires no account, does not collect a child's name, email or phone on any server, and never sends your child's learning data anywhere. No advertising, no external links in the kids' app, no selling of data.

1. Who we are

NikoLearn (the "Service") is a Georgian learning app for children, operated by its owner ("we"). Contact us at shonia.g@gmail.com or WhatsApp +995 593 255 385.

2. Data that stays on the device (and is never sent)

Your child's profile (the name you optionally enter), progress, scores and settings are stored only in that device's browser (localStorage). This data never reaches us. You can delete it at any time by removing the profile or clearing browser data.

3. What we collect (limited, anonymous)

  • Cloudflare Web Analytics (on the site and app pages): cookieless, no personal data, does not store IP addresses. It only counts visits and general usage (country, device type, number of visitors). It cannot identify your child.
  • Our own learning statistics (in the app): we collect only aggregate, anonymous counts to understand which subjects children use most (e.g. math, alphabet). No name, age, answer or IP address is ever sent. Cookieless. Data is processed on our own endpoint, with no third-party tracker.
  • Microsoft Clarity (marketing landing page only, NOT the kids' app): aggregate, anonymous statistics for parents/marketing.

We use no ad networks, do no profiling, and share no data about a child with third parties.

4. Children's privacy

NikoLearn is intended for children, so protecting them is non-negotiable.

United States (COPPA)

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Because we collect no personal information from a child, none is shared with third parties.

EU/EEA (GDPR, incl. Art. 8 on children)

We process no personal data of children; our analytics are aggregate and non-identifying. The lawful basis for web analytics is legitimate interest in aggregate statistics, with no profiling. A parent/guardian may contact us to exercise their rights.

Georgia (Law on Personal Data Protection)

We act in accordance with Georgia's Law on Personal Data Protection. As we process no personal data of a child, processing is limited to anonymous, aggregate statistics.

5. Parental control and rights

All of a child's data is on your device and fully under your control: delete the profile or clear browser data. For any question or a request to remove data, contact us at the address above.

6. Data retention

On-device data is kept until you delete it. Analytics are aggregate and retained per the relevant provider's policy.

7. Security

The Service runs over an encrypted connection (HTTPS). There is no central database of children's data that could be breached: the data stays on the device.

8. Changes

If we update this policy, we will change the date above.
